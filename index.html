Hi Moms
Medical Cares
The idea of HiMoms actually came up from the brief, where women is the main user that use this product and especially mothers!

So yup I came up with Hi-Moms. Basically “Hi” means the first phrase that actually everyone use it, so in other words mom as the prior. Then circle is the systems of all the life in the earth & the last one a bunch of women that will help each others on the product environment.

DECIDING ON
A STRATEGY

Initially, my main focus points were:

• Answer how far they are in their pregnancy journey.
• Connect them to their primary care physician for urgent matters.
• Potentially connect them with other mom.
‍
The first objective was to be achieved by creating simple widget when they just login/landed at home screen. Then to accomplish the second goal, I emphasized the accessibility on the navbar to create a system of different navigation to triggers users’ attention and to give an easy access in their urgency. And third bring the purpose of social media to the product, but in different way.

Actually I feel there is something missing on the brief; which is lack of monetizing. Well, if the client is local clinic, it means they have product/service to be used by user. So, I took initiative to add "clinic exposure as the brief". To cover up the clinic exposure, I use the home screen to show what are the product and services that clinic has it.

Design Story

Idea Summary

Creating pivotal clinic care that can break the status quo and share some memories between fellow members in the clinic itself to satisfied the individuals and businesses through curated connections in clinic’s product ecosystem

Product Idea

At the core of the business was a belief that ‘Women’s thing is matter’ had become a cliché phrase, but that the impact that I would try to bring here is such an authentic way to prove that the product can cover it all, when its delivered correctly, had a grace to it.

Well, let’s start how I came up with this. First thing first, it says that the clinic has a limited amount to cover everything about what women needs, not in the services or products but through emotional. I think women needs emotional support from the other, especially for the first mom that actually has anxious or worry when pregnancy or maybe not knowing how to treat their first child, and many things. Well, when the product is curated to have such a same pattern, which means all members is women. It makes the product is easily to be used.

So, the next move is to make the product looks more aesthetic but didn’t forget about the essence is smooth experience. Based on the research women are like to stay at the clean and beauty places rather than man. And to create a best product, we need to let user experienced the best UI with some simplicity and modern style with some micro-interaction, to make it feels is alive, memorable, and playful

So I wanted to emphasise what actually can I invent on some design possibilities with combining social media that actually worked as emotional towards clinic that worked based on the product and services. This led to ‘The Beauty of the Clinic Innovation’, which then moved me to create the product more aligned with the aesthetic aspects and memorable moments between the users.

TYpeface Research

Font Research

The reason I do research on typeface is because the product will going to be used on daily basis so the product will has heavy text, by I mean heavy test is most of the font gonna be used for product description etc.

So I think I need to match the product with the sense of typography itself. And I found several option, such as; Gili sans, Graphik, Aeonik also Neue Montreal.

And my consideration when I researched the typeface is about how in line the font with product.

Because what I’m trying to emphasise in the product is modern style, minimal, clean and playful, then I found two option Aeonik and Neue Montreal.

I choose the Neue Montreal, the needs of the product is not too formal and also the price is cheaper than Aeonik if we compared. But both of the is great!

Design System

Now when the logic proved to be working perfectly, I started developing a design system. The palette we chose comprised rather subtle colors, as I was to stick to brand colors. The and using grid system in terms of consistency. In doing so, we created a visible hierarchy of elements by using colors. We also created a series of fresh unique illustrations.

The design system is inspired by Fluent Design System and Human Interface guidelines. Especially the glass-morphism is cool to be implemented from those design system.

Also the purpose of design system is to make the process of development is way more efficient than without this. Start from the button, forms, tags and elements are design to be more sophisticated than the rest.

Yup when I've tried to make the design system inline with the look and feel about the product that came up with "modern style, aesthetic, simplicity, playful and memorable". So detail is crucial on this part, because we wanted to be more different than the rest. So that's why iconography was created by patience, due to make the product looks authentic.
