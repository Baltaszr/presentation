DECIDING ON The
art direction

Before I start to design, I ask myself what kind of look and feel that I want to emphasise on this website. So I came up to decide the first experience, yup loading. I think the first experience when loading the website is very important. It’s not only guide the use that this website is loading, but to set up the identity of the website and give the art direction a way more interesting.

Initially, our two main focus points were:

To make it highly visible of the art direction;
To impress people with everything that we have been worked;

Bringing typography is make a highly different than the rest of production website in indonesia also interaction/animation could make people impress with the worked that we have done.

Challenges

We analyzed the data and found that the target audience of the site is business owner & companies, who are eager to make the video of their company needs, but not knowledgeable enough for picking the good style for their needs. Most of them need the help of a good director to direct their company profile or the product/services. With the some portfolio that Fleava has it, with some companies or even individuals.

Concept Execution

After we came up with the art direction and done with analyzation the target audience then we trying to figure it out the two main points that we have highlights.

So yup we define the concept and designing the website, because the key of the concept of the art direction was to bring the identity on the website. Well, I didn’t want to just follow the trend but every element, grid, components and colors that I have been decide is reflected the brand identity to make the website is stand out.

I also have a special touch on the time easing when it comes to some transition and micro-interaction, to let people understand that we are different and creative than the rest by bringing WebGL into the website.

The design is driven by great design, our passion to create outstanding work, curiosity and excitement. Always brave. Always creative. Always challenging the status quo. The website should be reflected in Fleava Production’s brand identity.
